A healthy mix of quinoa, roasted vegetables, chickpeas, and avocado dressing.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup quinoa
- 1/2 cup roasted vegetables

## Instructions

1. Roast the vegetables.
2. Cook the quinoa.
3. Add the roasted vegetables to the quinoa.
